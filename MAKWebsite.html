Frequently Asked Questions(FAQs)

What are the mall opening hours?
The mall is open from 10 AM to 10 PM every day.
Is parking available?
Yes, we have ample parking space for all visitors.
Is there a feeding room available in the mall?
Yes, the mall provides a comfortable and private feeding room for mothers. It is equipped with seating, changing facilities, and a peaceful atmosphere for breastfeeding.
Is there a prayer room available for visitors?
Yes, there is a prayer room available in the mall for both men and women. The room is designed to provide a peaceful and clean environment for visitors to pray.
Will there be a play area for kids in the mall?
Yes,its under construction
Does the mall have a food court?
Yes, the mall features a spacious food court offering a wide variety of cuisines. Whether you're craving fast food, traditional meals, or healthy options, there's something for everyone.
Is the mall family-friendly?
Absolutely! Kohinoor Mall is designed with families in mind. With amenities like the food court, and comfortable seating areas, it's an ideal destination for family outings. The mall's layout and facilities cater to the needs of visitors of all ages.
Can I book shops or kiosks for business?
Yes, Mall of Kohinoor provides rental spaces for shops, andkiosks. Contact mall management for leasing details.
Are pets allowed in the mall?
Pets are not allowed inside the mall.
